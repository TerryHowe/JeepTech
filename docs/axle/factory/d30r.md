# Dana 30 Rear

![Dana 30 diff cover](../../img/axle/bwd30.jpg "Dana 30 diff cover")

A Dana 30 rear end was used briefly in the second generation Jeepster/Commando. It was replaced with a Dana 44 with two-piece shafts.
