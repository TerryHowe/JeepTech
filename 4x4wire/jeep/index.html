Welcome to JeepWire.com! The JeepWire.com staff is dedicated to bringing you the information you need to help you get the most out of your Jeep vehicle. Our goal is to cover the technical aspects of your Jeep including maintenance, restoration, modification, and part identification. We will also bring you coverage of Jeep events around the world, Jeep history, and Jeep trivia.

We will cover all things Jeep by recruiting staff members that live in different parts of the world and/or own different models of Jeep vehicles. We will also look for people that that enjoy different aspects of jeeping.

Honestly though, there is no way our staff can cover all the things we want to cover by ourselves. That is why we will be asking our readers to cover events for us and we are always on the look out for good technical articles. If you think you might have a good idea for an article, feel free to contact the JeepWire Managing Editor.

Thanks for stopping by!
