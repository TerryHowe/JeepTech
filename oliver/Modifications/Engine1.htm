The Jeep came with a Ford 2.3L four cylinder engine out of an '80 Pinto when I got it.  I actually helped with this swap long before I owned it.  The carbureted  2.3L is rated at 88 hp and is a big improvement over the 60 hp L134 that originally came in CJ-3As.  This particular engine ran Ok, but was starting to get a bit tired.  It smoked a bit, used and leaked oil, and was sometimes difficult to start.  The installation also left a bit to be desired, so I decided to replace it.
