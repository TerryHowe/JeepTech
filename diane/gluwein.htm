Glüwein Holiday Party

Chill Out and Warm Up for the Holidays

Come and Enjoy Some Hot Holiday Cheer

Wednesday, Dec 18th 7pm

 

 

I’ve been told that the best way to enjoy Glüwein (mulled wine) is after being outside.  Then, you can really enjoy the heated, spiced wine.  So first we’ll enjoy a moonlight hike in Garden of the Gods, then it’s back to Diane and Terry Howe’s for Glüwein.

 

When: Wed, December 18th.  Hike is at 7pm and Glüwein at The Howe’s is at 8pm.

 

Where:  Meet at the main parking lot in GOG for a moonlight stroll at 7pm. The main parking lot is IN the park, enter from 30th St, and follow the One-Way about 1/8th of the way around the park. 

After 8, meet at Diane and Terry Howe’s, 670 Glen Eyrie Circle, Colorado Springs. 

Glen Eyrie Circle is on the west side, off of 30th, between Fontmore (Fillmore) and Uintah.
